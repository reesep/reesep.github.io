Lecture Outline

  • We will be writing our own sorting algorithms. Today's lecture focuses on Bubble sort
  • Bubble sort runs in O(n^2) time (n = array size). This is because we have a nested for loop
  • Bubble sort involves comparing pairs of adjacent values, and swapping. This causes large numbers ("bubbles") to rise to the top
