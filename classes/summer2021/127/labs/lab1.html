Background and Directions

You will create an ASCII picture of an unknown boat out at sea. Then, you will create a Python program that will prompt the user for (1) the name of the boat and (2) the year the picture was created. Afterwards, you will display the picture as well as a caption. The caption will say "S.S." followed by the boat name, and year (see required output).

You will need to create your own ASCII boat or find one on the internet and put it in a box, just like the following art below:
